What is this site?

Computer Science and Music are two different studies. I work on personal projects in both of them, and it's tricky to decide where to put them. I can put my CS projects on github, which is fine. The question is, what about music? I can put it on YouTube, but I can't put my CS projects there. I found middle grounds making a website and uploading my work to respective pages.
